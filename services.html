Our Services

Helping you bridge the communication gap with British Sign Language.

BSL Interpreting Services

The role of a BSL Interpreter is to convey spoken language into British Sign Language (BSL) to ensure effective communication. BSL interpreters help deaf and hearing people communicate with each other in various contexts, including:

  • Business meetings
  • Events and conferences
  • Medical appointments
  • Educational purposes

We provide highly skilled interpreters who are available for a wide range of services. Whether for a formal setting or a casual interaction, we ensure communication is clear and effective.

Specialized Deaf Awareness Training

We offer tailored deaf awareness training for professionals in different sectors, including:

  • Healthcare
  • Education
  • Retail
  • Families and Friends

Our training ensures that professionals are equipped to interact with deaf clients, students, patients, or colleagues in a respectful and effective manner.

Roger Pen and Radio Aid

We also provide Roger Pen/Radio Aid systems to make it easier for deaf clients to interact with their surroundings. This technology helps deaf individuals to hear better in different environments, making it a valuable tool for those who are hard of hearing. It could be helpful for you when interacting with a deaf person in your day-to-day life.

BSL Courses

We offer a range of BSL courses, starting from Level 1 (beginner) to Level 6 (advanced). Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to improve your skills, we have the right course for you.

Additionally, we offer a free trial for a short duration, allowing potential clients to experience our courses before committing to the full program. This is an opportunity for you to see if our courses fit your learning goals.

Empowering Deaf People

Our goal is to empower individuals and groups by ensuring they have access to clear and full information. We work with both individuals and organizations to enable deaf people to have their voices heard and ensure effective communication. We believe in the importance of British Sign Language and are also campaigning for legal protections and raising awareness about its significance.

Event and Conference Interpreting

We also provide event and conferencing interpreting services for those who wish to hire BSL interpreters for their clients or events. Whether it's for a corporate event, conference, or meeting, we ensure that your event is inclusive and accessible for deaf individuals.
